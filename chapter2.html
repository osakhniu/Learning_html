The Story in the Book

Chapter 1

Moly had been staring out of her window for about an hour now. On her desk, lying between the copies of her Nature New Scientist, and all other scientific journals her work had appeared in, was a well thumbed copy of On the road. It had been Moly's favourite book since college and the longer she spent in these four walls the more she felt she needed to be free.

She had spen the last 10 years in this room sitting under a poster with an Oscar Wild's quote proclaiming that Work is the refuge of people who have nothing better to do. Although many considered her pioneering work, unraveling the secrets of the llama DNA, to be an outstanding achievement, Moly did think sha had something better to do.
