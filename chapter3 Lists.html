Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is the recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and a cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter had melted add cream and eggs
